Welcome to JU College

John University is committed to excellence in teaching, learning, and science, as well as the development of world leaders. Johns University graduates have made important and innovative contributions to culture, the arts and sciences, industry, and national and foreign relations for more than three and a half decades. The liberal arts and sciences are the focus of the four-year, full-time undergraduate program. Undergraduates usually take four classes each semester in order to graduate in the traditional four years. Honors degrees in most fields include specialized coursework and a senior thesis. Despite the fact that certain beginner classes have broad class sizes, the average class size is 12 students.
